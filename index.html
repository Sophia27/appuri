At a glance

一見してわかること

Offline Map (Most useful feature)

Japan can be a tricky place – anyone who has ever tried to find a Japanese address knows. It’s hard! If you aren’t able to speak or read anything and wifi is not available, you can get lost in translation easily. Furthermore Japanese cities are among the densest places in the world. Some neighborhoods are so multi-layered, flashy, and confusing – how are you supposed to know, that the best tonkatsu is served in that ten-story building, two floors beneath the ground, around the corner to the left, behind that simple wooden door with a minimal sign? That’s when an offline map and access description proves really useful.

Glossary (Coolest feature)

Dig deeper into the Japanese culinary universe, by reading short glossar articles about lesser known ingredients, dishes and other fun facts.

Design

At the core we are designers – so to us the visual appearance of the app is as important as the content. You will not be disturbed by adds or anything like it. It’s all about presenting these places from their most beautiful side and nothing else.

Useful Insights

Find out what kind of Japanese food is served in the restaurant and what the people, who recommended the place love most about it. And besides that get useful tipps on specialities, prices, opening hours, and how to contact.
